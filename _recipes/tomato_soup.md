steps_en:
  - title: How to Cook
    actions:
      - Wash, peel, and cut the vegetables into large pieces.
      - Add all the ingredients from the preparation section to a pressure cooker and cook for one whistle.
      - Allow it to cool, then blend until smooth.
      - Transfer the soup back to the same cooker or pan and cook uncovered for 5 minutes. Adjust the consistency with water if it is too thick.
      - The soup is ready to serve. Serve warm with butter or fresh cream.
