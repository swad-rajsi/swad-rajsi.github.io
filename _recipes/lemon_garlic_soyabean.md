steps_en:
  - title: Preparation
    actions:
      - Wash, peel and cut vegetables as instructed and keep aside.
      - Boil water in a sauce pan till rolling boil.
      - Add soya chunks in it and boil for 7 minutes.
      - Drain them and put in bowl of cold water for 5 minutes to stop cooking process. 
      - Squeeze water out of soya chunks and keep aside.

  - title: Cooking
    actions:
      - Add oil in a wok or pan on medium flame.
      - Add cumin seeds, crushed garlic, crushed ginger and saute for a minute until fragrant but not burnt.
      - Add chopped onion, bellpepper/ capsicum and saute for two minutes.
      - Add chopped tomatoes and saute till tomatoes turn soft.
      - Add turmeric, chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder and dry mango powder.
      - Saute well till oil starts to release.
      - Now, add soy chunks, lemon juice and salt. Toss well on medium heat for five minutes.
      - Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves. Serve warm.
